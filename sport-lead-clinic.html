Learn to Lead on Real Rock

The Sport Lead Climbing Clinic bridges the gap between top-rope and leading outdoors. You'll spend the day on permitted routes in the Red River Gorge, working through mock leading progressions, clipping sequences, and safe falling: the skills that unlock the full range of outdoor climbing.

🧗 What You'll Learn

  • Mock leading and progressive lead drills
  • Efficient bolt clipping technique
  • Controlled and safe falling practice
  • GriGri and ATC lead belaying
  • Route reading and clipping strategies

🎒 What to Bring

  • Minimum 2 liters of water
  • Weather-appropriate layers
  • Crag lunch and snacks
  • Sleeping system rated to 40°F if camping
  • Your own shoes, harness, or helmet if you prefer to use personal gear

✅ What's Provided

  • All climbing gear included: ropes, harness, helmet, GriGri/ATC, quickdraws, and anchor equipment
  • AMGA-certified instruction throughout

📍 Where We Climb

  • RRGCC land (Red River Gorge Climbers' Coalition)
  • Muir Valley (private preserve)
  • Crag chosen based on conditions & level

👤 Prerequisites

  • Comfortable top-roping outdoors or in a gym
  • Can tie a figure-eight on a bight
  • Basic understanding of belay commands

⏱️ Duration & Group Size

  • Full day: 8–9 hours
  • Max 6 participants per guide
  • Private and semi-private sessions available

📍 Meetup: Miguel's Pizza campground, Slade, KY 40376

🏕️ Camping: On-site camping available at Miguel's. Breakfast and dinner can be purchased there.

🎯 Best For: Top-rope climbers ready to take the next step, gym climbers wanting to translate skills outdoors, and anyone building toward outdoor trad or multi-pitch goals
