## 3. Services & Operating Region
* **Primary Region:** Red River Gorge, Kentucky (Powell, Wolfe, Menifee, and Lee Counties).
* **Secondary Region:** Select areas in Indiana.
* **Permitted Activities (What we do):** Top-rope climbing, single-pitch sport and traditional climbing guiding, anchor-building instruction, mock leading, and rappelling.
* **Exclusions (What we do NOT do):** No multi-pitch guiding.

## 4. Package Concepts & Pricing Baseline
*(Note to AI: Use these to replace the RV "Vehicles" and "Nightly Rates" with Climbing Packages)*
* **Intro to Outdoor Climbing (Half Day / Full Day):** Focuses on top-rope climbing, basic movement technique, tying figure-eight knots, and belay basics.
* **Sport Lead Climbing Clinic:** Focuses on mock leading, clipping bolts, falling safely on lead, and using a GriGri or ATC. 
* **Trad & Anchor Building:** Focuses on gear placement (cams/nuts), evaluating natural features, and building redundant, equalized anchors.
* **Group/Collegiate Trips:** Accommodating university climbing clubs (e.g., 1.5-day intensive climbing weekends). *Reference pricing: Custom group rates available (past baseline: ~$70/head for 1.5 days for large university groups).*

## 5. Client Logistics & Expectations
*(Note to AI: Use this to replace RV "Vehicle Amenities" or "What's Included" sections)*
* **Accommodation:** Guided trips often basecamp at Miguel’s Pizza campground. Clients must be prepared for tent/hammock camping and temperature drops.
* **Client Required Gear:** Minimum 2 liters of water, weather-appropriate clothing, sleeping system (rated for 40°F/4°C if camping), climbing shoes, and a climbing helmet.
* **Optional/Provided Gear:** Harness, GriGri, Quickdraws, PAS. (Crag College provides ropes and all primary safety/anchor equipment).
* **Food:** Clients generally pack their own crag lunches; breakfast/dinner can be purchased at Miguel's Pizza.

## 6. Tone and Brand Voice
* **Professional yet Approachable:** The tone should balance the rigorous safety standards of an AMGA-certified guide with the welcoming, educational vibe of a "College" for climbers. 
* **Educational:** Copy should focus on progression, teaching best practices (Leave No Trace, proper belay technique), and building client independence.
* **Terminology to Use:** Crag, single-pitch, sport, trad, top-rope, anchors, belay, approach, send.
